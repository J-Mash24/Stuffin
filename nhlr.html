I wanted to try something new, so I tried to use R instead of Python. It was a while after my last try with R, so here are some basic calculations and charts. It was a bit easier, than using Python. Like 10% more simple.

Here I counted some NHL stats from last season. Main focus was on goals scored and goals allowed.

I compared expected goals and actual goals scored. After that I counted the difference and looked which team outscored it's expectations.

For ex, "Sabres" and "Blue Jackets" scored more goals, than xG showed. "Flyers" allowed more goals, than they were expected. Going back to the charts, It's not that impressive, but it took some time to remember things.
